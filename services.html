Our Services

More about Our Services

Sunday Services

Every Sunday

1:00PM to 3:00PM

RCCG The Chapel Outreach Norwich, Colman Rd, NR4 7HD

This is our weekly service where we come together to worship the Lord as a family. We incorporate worship, praise, Sunday School and a powerful sermon.

We begin with Sunday School, where we dig into the Bible enabling people to get a greater understanding of the Word of God, which is so integral to our faith.

We also offer a strong Children’s Church and Youth Ministry, where young people are supported and encouraged in their walk with God through Bible Lessons, arts and crafts and important skill development.

We hope to see you at our service!

Please Note: Ministers and workers are expected to be at the venue by 12:00pm

Tuesday Bible Study

Every Tuesday

7:00PM to 9:00PM

RCCG The Chapel Outreach Norwich, Colman Rd, NR4 7HD

This is our weekly service when we come together to study the Word of God.

In this service, we delve into the bible and try to answer any questions you may have.

We hope to see you at our service!

First Friday Ignite Prayer

Every First Friday of the Month

9:00PM to 11:00PM

RCCG The Chapel Outreach Norwich, Colman Rd, NR4 7HD

This is a service which runs on the First Friday of every month.

In this service, we come together and thank God for the month we have passed through, and declare for the month ahead.

We hope to see you at our service!

Bible Feast

Every Third Tuesday of the Month

7:00PM to 9:00PM

RCCG The Chapel Outreach Norwich, Colman Rd, NR4 7HD

Bible Feast is a special service, running on the Third Tuesday of every month.

This service runs similar to our Bible Study. However, at the beginning of this service, we share a meal together.

Afterwards, we discuss a contemporary topic as a family, and how it relates to the bible.

We hope to see you at our service!

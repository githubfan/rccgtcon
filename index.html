Welcome to a Family

The Chapel Outreach Norwich is a Christian Church serving the Norwich community and seeking, engaging, and encouraging others in their life-changing Christian journey.


We seek to be a loving, friendly community that worships God, and serves others. We place a high priority on teaching from the Bible and following the example of Jesus.


Our vision is to impact and renew Norwich and beyond with the transforming message of Jesus Christ through words and actions.


Come as you are - we'd love to get to know you.
